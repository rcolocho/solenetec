What is Green Button?

Green Button is a secure federal standard that lets you download or share up to 13 months of your actual electricity usage data directly from your utility. All three major California utilities — SDG&E, SCE, and PG&E — support it. Using your real data gives you a far more accurate estimate than guessing.
